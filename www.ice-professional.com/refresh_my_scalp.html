    • TONIC
    FOR ALL HAIR TYPES
    Provides stimulating action to scalp, strengthens hair folicles, stimulates healthy hair growth & prevents hair loss. Restores hair, making it stronger & shinier.

    DIRECTIONS: apply directly to dry scalp & massage gently with circular motion. Do not rinse.
